About this website

This website itself is one of my favorite projects! I have always loved design but focused my abilities on decorating my room. Interior design, whether that be choosing cozy duvet covers and stuffed animals or fun light sources like my sunset lamp, is a creative way for me to relax and build a cozy, safe space. I took this interest to web design and decided to build a virtual portfolio. I feel like I have so much to share, and I can't fit all of it on a one page resume. We are all talented, complex individuals that deserve a space to showcase our talents and abilities. That is exactly what this website is for me! I hope you love it as much as I do.

As I continue to expand my technical skills, I am sure I will come up new and innovative personal projects to share with you all. They will live here, so come back to check it out!
