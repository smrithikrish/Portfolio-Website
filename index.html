Who is Smrithi?

Smrithi is a Statistics and Machine Learning student at Carnegie Mellon University, passionate about using data for public health and social impact.

She’s worked on projects ranging from child brain development to healthcare access in Allegheny County, with a growing interest in how data science can advance equity in real-world systems.

Smrithi has experience with R, SQL, C, and Python- as well as an interest in casual inference and experiment design. She enjoys working with messy datasets, using visualization tools and statistical modeling techniques to uncover patterns that matter.
